"Lenten Recollection at JNRAL Family Corp. Hospital"

-March 14, 2024

Reflecting on Sacrifice and Resurrection
This Lenten season, JNRAL Family Corp. Hospital invites everyone to reflect on the profound sacrifices of Jesus on the cross. Though we may feel unworthy of His forgiveness, God's mercy leads us to the joy of Easter and resurrection. As Luke 15:24 reminds us, “For this son of mine was dead and is alive again; he was lost and is found!”
A heartfelt thank you to the medical practitioners and staff of JNRAL Family Corp. Hospital.
