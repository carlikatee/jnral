"Successful Operation Tuli in Bancaan, Naic"

-June 15, 2024

A Community Effort
On June 15, 2024, an Operation Tuli event was successfully conducted in Bancaan, Naic, thanks to the dedication and hard work of all the volunteers. This community-driven initiative provided essential services to young boys, reflecting the spirit of cooperation and care within the community. The efforts of the volunteers made this event possible, ensuring a positive experience for all involved.
