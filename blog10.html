Ensuring Safety and Preparedness
JNRAL Family Corp Hospital conducted a fire drill to enhance safety and preparedness among staff and patients. The drill was an important exercise in emergency response, ensuring that everyone is equipped to act swiftly and effectively in case of a fire. This proactive approach reflects the hospital's commitment to maintaining a safe environment for all.
