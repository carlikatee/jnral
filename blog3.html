"Strengthening Healthcare Partnerships in Cavite"

-June 18-19, 2024

DOH, Private Health Facilities, and Legislators Unite

On June 18-19, 2024, an important orientation took place at Alta D' Tagaytay, bringing together stakeholders from the Department of Health, private health facilities, and local legislators.

The event focused on reinforcing partnerships to enhance healthcare services across Cavite. Discussions centered on collaborative strategies to address healthcare challenges and improve service delivery. The gathering was a significant step toward a more unified and effective healthcare system in the region.
