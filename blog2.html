"JNRAL Family Corporation Celebrates 29 Years of Success"

-August 1, 2024

Team Building at Hardin ng Postema

JNRAL Family Corp. marked its 29th anniversary with a memorable celebration and team-building event at Hardin ng Postema on August 1, 2024. The day was filled with activities that strengthened bonds and fostered camaraderie among team members.

Special thanks to the organizing committee: Raul Manas, Vhie Cervana, Elmie Navalatan, Ate Eunice Marcial Gallardo, Raquel M. Caparas, Laica Placides, Leonor Deguzmansangalang, Gladys Cenizal Omel, Elizabeth Elisabeth Geroy, and Mark Ivan Clark Samartino.

The event highlighted the spirit of unity that drives JNRAL’s continued success.
